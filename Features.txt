PBAMember
    Typical Features:
        Key Information & Features on HOA Websites:
        Governing Documents: Access to Covenants, Conditions, and Restrictions (CC&Rs), bylaws, and articles of incorporation.
        Community Management: News, announcements, event calendars, and updates on maintenance schedules.
        Resident Portal: Secure, personal accounts for paying dues online, viewing account balances, and submitting requests.
        Forms and Submissions: Digital forms for architectural review requests (ARC), maintenance complaints, and violation reporting.
        Financial & Meeting Info: Approved annual budgets, financial reports, and minutes from board meetings.
        Amenities & Directory: Rules for common areas, reservation systems for clubhouses or pools, and a community directory.
